01 — What is this data?

LIGO and Virgo have detected gravitational waves from 219 compact binary mergers — mostly black hole pairs spiraling into each other at near-light speed. The catalog (GWTC) includes component masses, spins, and the total energy radiated as gravitational waves. Each event converts a few percent of the system's mass directly into spacetime ripples, exactly as Einstein predicted in 1915.

We gave ProtoScience the catalog of measured masses and energies. No general relativity. No mention of chirp mass. Just numbers from the most extreme events in the universe.
